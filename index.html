Privacy Policy

Data Collection

I collect location data only with your explicit permission to provide accurate weather information for your area. This data is never stored permanently or shared with third parties.

Data Usage

Location data is used solely for weather forecasting purposes. I fetch weather data from Open-Meteo API based on your location coordinates. No personal information is collected or stored.

Local Storage

Only preferences like theme selection and sound settings are stored locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time.

Cookies

I use minimal browser storage only for app preferences. No tracking cookies or analytics are implemented to respect your privacy.

Data Usage Policy

Weather Data Sources

My weather data comes from Open-Meteo, a professional meteorological service that aggregates data from multiple national weather services worldwide.

I use IP geolocation as a fallback and GPS when permitted for precise location detection. GPS data provides street-level accuracy for weather conditions.

All weather calculations and visualizations are performed client-side in your browser. No weather data is stored on our servers.

Help & FAQ

‚ùì How do I search for a location?

Type any address, city, or landmark in the search box at the top. I support street-level accuracy worldwide. Simply start typing and select from the suggested results.

üìÖ How do I change the date and time?

Click on any day in the 7-day forecast to see that day's weather. Use the time navigation controls or click on hourly forecast cards to explore different times. Click "Today" or the logo to return to current conditions.

üå¶Ô∏è What do the animations mean?

Background animations reflect real weather conditions - rain shows actual precipitation intensity, clouds cover the sun when cloudy, and lightning appears during thunderstorms. The sun position changes based on time of day.

üìä How accurate is the data?

I use Open-Meteo API which provides professional-grade weather data updated hourly from multiple national meteorological services. Accuracy is typically very high for current conditions and decreases slightly for longer forecasts.

üì± Does it work offline?

Recent weather data is cached for offline viewing for up to 5 minutes. For fresh data, an internet connection is required.

üéµ How do I control sound effects?

Click the sound icon in the header to toggle weather sound effects on or off. Sounds include rain, wind, and thunder based on current conditions.

üåô How do I switch themes?

Click the theme toggle button (moon/sun icon) in the header to switch between light and dark themes. Your preference is saved automatically.

üìç Why do you need location access?

Location access provides precise weather data for your exact position. I use IP-based location by default, but GPS provides more accurate results. Location data is never stored or shared.
